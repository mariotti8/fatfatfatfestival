LOUIE

VEGA

(US)
SUNDAY, 4TH AUGUST - MAIN STAGE, GRANCIA DI SARROCCIANO
The Bronx, the mid-60s: Louie Vega is just a child when his mother takes him to the Madison Square Garden to see his uncle Hector Lavoe's concert, one of the most important musicians in the history. Fast-forward: the disco music outbreak, the first parties organized by Afrika Bambaataa, the hip hop birth. In love with the music and his message, Vega attends as many parties as possible and is inspired by the epic nature of the various clubs in New York. His first residency in 1984, the meeting with Kenny "Dope" Gonzalez, the Masters At Work (and the Nuyorican Soul!) project. The rest is history. Today Louie Vega is a full member of the Olympus of Music.

The sensuality of the Purple King singing with desire “I want to be your lover”. The extreme and caricatured psychedelism of Funkadelic screaming “Oh, Funk me please!”. 03

MOTOR CITY

DRUM ENSEMBLE

(DE)
SATURDAY, 3RD AUGUST - MAIN STAGE, GRANCIA DI SARROCCIANO (b2b with Mr. Scruff)
Like a gentle, unkempt and a bit clumsy Murakami’s character, Motor City Drum Ensemble is truly passionate about small and daily healing joys: he love to cook, to swim and - of course - to dig into the collections of every record shop he visit around the world. Born and raised in the Stuttgart countryside, the young Danilo has shaped his artistic attitude listening the overseas black music, absorbing and developing the love message and freedom beliefs from the Chicago House and Detroit Techno movements. Thinking as a group and acting alone, with the ‘Raw Cuts’ series (2008) and the DJ-Kicks mix (2011) he defined his personal sound universe: a motley combination of cosmic jazz, afrobeat, deep house and dreamlike feelings. Something necessary in our life.

MR. SCRUFF

(UK)
SATURDAY, 3RD AUGUST - MAIN STAGE, GRANCIA DI SARROCCIANO (b2b with Motor City Drum Ensemble)
According with his family, friends and collaborators, Andy Charty is one of the funniest geek in the music world: an adorable guy with a big sense of humor, his cup of tea and a J Dilla nerd t-shirt. He has been Mr. Scruff for over nineteen years: deejay, record producer and illustrator, he drags you into his full-detailed imagination among sound engineering and ‘potato style’ cartoons. A living and walking music encyclopedia, willing to play anything can make happy everyone around him. It’s all about lingering gazes and common soulful feelings, an every-time different music journey to which you can join and letting you completely go.

AMP FIDDLER

(US)
FRIDAY, 2ND AUGUST - PIAZZA VITT. EMANUELE II, MORROVALLE with A DRUMMER FROM DETROIT
Amp Fiddler is a living legend. Easy to say. And it also may be possible to state that he has inspired - with unique versatility and open-mindedness - entires generations of incredible artists: J Dilla’s mentor, he also worked alongside George Clinton, Prince, Jamiroquai, Moodymann… It should be enough? Energy from funk, sweetness from soul, happiness from disco, power from techno: Amp Fiddler’s music feeds the mind, the heart and the spirit. He will debut at FAT FAT FAT with a special live show featuring A Drummer From Detroit (Dez Andrés).

06

DJ SPINNA

(US)
SUNDAY, 4TH AUGUST - MAIN STAGE, GRANCIA DI SARROCCIANO (b2b with GE-OLOGY)
Universally recognized as one of the most versatile and influential figures on the contemporary scene (Eminem, Stevie Wonder, Mos Def, De La Soul are just a few of the countless names on the list of artists with whom he has collaborated), Vincent Williams aka DJ Spinna - a Brooklyn native, a childhood spent to collect records - is something of an anomaly in music. A humbling example of what it takes to truly succeed within the framework of multiple fields. A steadfast work and obsessive research led him to understand, before many others, that the fusion between hip hop, jazz, soul and funk was possible and damn winning. Ignoring any restriction, he feels the DJ life as an ethical mission: Spinna plays always for the crowd and its satisfaction!

GE-OLOGY

(US)
SUNDAY, 4TH AUGUST - MAIN STAGE, GRANCIA DI SARROCCIANO (b2b with DJ SPINNA)
Breathing art and music, capturing vibes since before his own birth. Destiny has reserved to Ge-Ology a path of sudden breakthroughs with an unknown final destination. He always followed his deep inspiration and along his journey he met mythological figures such as Tupac, Mos Def and De La Soul. But there is so much more into the Gerard Young's story. There is something that can’t be defined by words. Something influenced by Baltimore and New York, by a family with an extraordinary passion for the music, by a background with its roots into the most important years of the modern music’s history. Producer, deejay and visual artist. This is Ge-Ology, but not only this. Listen to believe. Listen to love.

ANTAL

(NL)
SUNDAY, 4TH AUGUST - MAIN STAGE, GRANCIA DI SARROCCIANO
Rush Hour co-Founder Antal Heitlager prefers not to put a name to his style, the most he’ll concede is that his music is about a diversity that feels raw & spiritual. Antal - a deejay, a football coach and a father - is a compulsive music researcher, moved by instinct and determination. MOVE D

(DE)

SUNDAY, 4TH AUGUST - GIARDINO DELLA SGUGOLA, GRANCIA DI SARROCCIANO
A window on the outer space, the Kraftwerk’s electronic revolution as soundtrack, and a child who tries to build a spaceship on his own. As a kid, David Moufang dreamed the cosmos watching ’2001: A Space Odyssey’; growing up he imagined to write its personal idea of the score: from the deepest techno to the most loving funk. As an artist - with his several amazing projects - Move D put himself in the middle of the two “sides”. And after almost three decades he continue to stay there, undconditionally loving the music and the stars.

OPTIMO

(UK)
SATURDAY, 3RD AUGUST - MAIN STAGE, GRANCIA DI SARROCCIANO (b2b with MOVE D)
‘Optimo’: a tribute to a crazy track by Liquid Liquid (1983) where batucada, garage house, hip hop and funk collide all together; and the deepest celebration of the club culture made in Scotland as thought by JD Twitch and Jonnie Wilkes. Glasgow based game-changing project born at the end of the 90’s, Optimo is the uncompromising reaction to the all night long 4/4 beat boredom. From rock psychedelia, through punk aesthetics, to rave feelings, it is the incessant gamble of your subconscious able to summarize all the sounds just in a extremely intense moment.
